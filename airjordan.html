Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG 'Black and Gold'
$1600.00

The Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG ‘Black and Gold’ sees the iconic silhouette take on a flashy aesthetic, thanks to a full patent leather build. The glossy material is complemented by a simple two-tone finish, highlighted by a black base with metallic gold accents on the Swoosh, ‘Wings’ logo, and tongue tag. The partially translucent gum rubber outsole is sprinkled with gold glitter for added style. Modeled after an obscure 1985 original colorway, this Black/Metallic Gold Jordan 1 was one of the most sought after Jordans in history, with no confirmation on where it was released or even how it was distributed. The original pairs still command grail status even today.

Supreme x Air Jordan 5 Retro 'Black'
$700.00

When the Jordan Brand posted a photo of Michael Jordan (shot by Terry Richardson) wearing a Jordan and Supreme branded t-shirt on their Instagram, fans went crazy. Rumors surrounding a Supreme Air Jordan 5 were true. Dropped in October of 2015 in three colorways — White, Black, and Desert Camo — the sneaker was the first and only brand collaboration for the Air Jordan 5 line. Released alongside a small capsule collection of clothing, the sneaker features Supreme branding across the netting and the number ‘94’ on the heel representing the year Supreme was founded.
